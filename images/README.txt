Idriss Auto — optional category photos
======================================

Drop square/landscape JPG images here to show a photo banner at the top of each
catalog category. The app works fine without them (it falls back to an orange
gradient banner). Each image appears only on its own category screen.

Recommended: ~800x500px or larger, JPG, under ~200 KB each.

Exact filenames the app looks for:

  cat-engine.jpg        -> Engine
  cat-brakes.jpg        -> Brakes
  cat-filters.jpg       -> Filters
  cat-electrical.jpg    -> Electrical
  cat-suspension.jpg    -> Suspension
  cat-ignition.jpg      -> Ignition
  cat-cooling.jpg       -> Cooling
  cat-belts.jpg         -> Belts & Chains
  cat-transmission.jpg  -> Transmission
  cat-exhaust.jpg       -> Exhaust

If your file is a PNG, edit data.js and change that category's "img" value to
match your real filename.
